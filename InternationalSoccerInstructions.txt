Instructions
To use this program:
* Download the zipped files SoccerData.zip and Winner.zip. Extract the files and ensure that SoccerData.csv and Winner.R are saved in Downloads.
* Open R and change the directory to Downloads
* Type “source(‘Winner.R’)” in R
* After pressing enter, type Winner followed by two international soccer teams: 
EX: Winner(“Canada”, “Italy”)
* The function will output the predicted winner of the international soccer match
EX: “Italy Wins!”
